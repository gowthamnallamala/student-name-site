Places to visit in Nellore

MGB Mall

This is one of the biggest malls in Nellore. And its currently the biggest and the only Mall which is currently open and completely function in Nellore. Its built by MG brothers. Daily thousands of people from all over the district visit here. There are many outlets of popular brands such as Dominos, Pizzahut, McDonalds, PUMA, CreamStone, Reliance trends, MAX clothing, WROGN etc. Along with this there are five theatres along with various food courts and gaming. Its a overall fun Places blended with everything for fun. Even there are various rides specially for kids. A must visit place

Rain Square Mall

This gonna be the biggest mall once it starts functioning. Currently only the movie theatres are functinal. Its built with great infrastructure with lot more brand outlets. Food court outlets are also open now, which is a fun place to hangout for movies and food currently. It has 5 movie theatres with great interiors. Its around 2-3 kms from APSRTC bus-stand and adjacent to KIMS hospital.

Mallikarjuna Kamakshi Temple Jonnawada

Its one of the oldest and largest temples in Nellore district. Its very famous in the region. Many people come especially for staying here for a night which is part of ritual when one's husaband dies. Its very sacred to the people around here. Its around 13kms from Nellore city. One can easily take auto/cab from Nellore. In the inner sanctum Lord Mallikarjuna is seen. Beside that shrine Goddess Kamakshi is seen in standing posture with a goad (ankusha) and a noose (pasha) in the upper pair of hands and showing Abhaya and Varada Mudras with her lower pair of hands. Adi Shankaracharya statue can also be seen here.

Nellapattu Sanctuary

Its a great sanctuary with an around 459 hectares. Around 189 different species can be found here. hese migratory birds include common teal, shoveler, Spot-bill duck, grey heron, black-winged stilt, and garganey gadwall etc. In 1997 GOI declared it as a sanctuary. Thousands of people visit here during a speacial season. Even there will be helicopter rides during that time as a tourist attraction provided by Tourism department.
