How to reach ?

Rahmatabad or A.S.Peta or Anumasamundram peta is situated in ASPETA mandal in Nellore district, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is well connected by road to major cities like Hyderabad, Bangalore and chennai.people in hyderabad can avail direct buses up to 7 buses daily. Its around 50 kms from Nellore. Various RTC and private bus operates are running sleeper/seater buses to this place.
